The core thesis of Module 2

You cannot out-diet a conditioned identity. You cannot out-exercise a chronic stress state.

The body's ability to regulate energy, build muscle, manage fat, sleep deeply, and repair itself is not determined solely by diet and exercise. It is determined by the signal quality of the psychological and emotional life, which is set by the identity structures running the SIGNAL cascade.

The 4Ps Framework

The four domains every metabolic conversation works at once

Every metabolic coaching conversation operates across four domains simultaneously. The coach who only addresses Physiology is practicing lifestyle medicine, not metabolic coaching. The NLH metabolic coach works all four at once.

Physiology
The biological mechanisms: hormones, nervous system, metabolic rate, inflammation, cellular function.
Psychology
The identity structures, stress patterns, and emotional climate that drive physiology.
Preferences
Individual variation in chronotype, food response, exercise tolerance, temperament. What works FOR this person.
Practicals
The real-world constraints: schedule, budget, relationships, lifestyle. What is actually executable.
DomainWithout it
PhysiologyMissing the body layer entirely
PsychologyLifestyle interventions produce limited, temporary results
PreferencesGeneric prescriptions that do not stick
PracticalsPerfect plans that do not run in reality

A plan that addresses only Physiology produces limited and temporary results. A plan that addresses Psychology without Physiology may produce insight without the biological substrate to support it. Both layers are required.

The Four Striving Archetypes

Metabolic profiles of conditioned identity

These are not personality types. They are motivational patterns driven by conditioned identity structures, each with a characteristic metabolic signature.

Striver
Achievement, performance, never-enough.
Seeker
Spiritual bypassing, perpetual searching, never arriving.
Soldier
Discipline, duty, pushing through.
Shamer
Avoiding judgment, hiding inadequacy, managing appearance.

Striver

Core MUD: "I am only as good as my last result. Stopping = failure."

Metabolic signature

  • Chronically elevated cortisol (adrenal dominance pattern)
  • Disrupted sleep, mind will not shut off as the DMN runs achievement loops
  • HEC dysregulation: energy crashes, cravings as cortisol compensation
  • Inflammation as the cost of sustained high-output operation
  • Burnout pattern: extended high cortisol followed by adrenal exhaustion

Recovery application: The high-performing man who outworks his problem. Willpower gets him sober, clean, consistent, until his system crashes. His recovery strategy is itself a striving behaviour. The work is identity-level: releasing the equation between performance and worth.

Seeker

Core MUD: "I haven't found the answer yet. When I find it, everything will change."

Metabolic signature

  • Hormonal dysregulation from inconsistency, every new protocol disrupts the last
  • Blood sugar instability from trend-chasing diet behaviour
  • Cortisol from the chronic background anxiety of not-yet-finding
  • Poor sleep quality from elevated mental activity and frequent protocol changes

Recovery application: Consumes recovery content without landing it. Sophisticated collection of insights, thin embodiment of any single one. The work is Retrain, accumulating reps rather than accumulating knowledge.

Soldier

Core MUD: "Hard is good. Rest is weakness. I can handle anything."

Metabolic signature

  • Overtrain and under-recover pattern
  • Chronic inflammation from insufficient recovery
  • Testosterone suppression (overtraining syndrome)
  • Injury cycle as the body forcing rest the identity will not permit

Recovery application: Less common in BMR, but appears when recovery itself becomes discipline performance.

Shamer

Core MUD: "I am fundamentally flawed. People must not see the real me."

Metabolic signature

  • Restrict-binge cycle: shame drives restriction, restriction drives binge, binge drives shame
  • Cortisol elevation from sustained shame-state (AFRAID emotional baseline)
  • Insulin dysregulation from the binge component
  • Body image as primary stressor, the body itself becomes a threat cue

Recovery application: The BMR primary archetype. The shame wound that drives compulsive sexual behaviour maps directly to this core MUD. Shame is inflammatory. Reducing shame is a direct metabolic intervention.

SHMEC and HEC Markers

The body's observable feedback system

SHMEC

Sleep / Hunger / Mood / Energy / Cravings.

  • When SHMEC is "in the box" (stable across all five), the body is metabolically balanced and the coaching is working at the biological level.
  • When SHMEC is dysregulated, identity-level work is needed, not another protocol change.

A client whose sleep, mood, and cravings stay dysregulated despite "doing everything right" has a conditioned identity running a SIGNAL cascade that is overriding the lifestyle interventions.

HEC

Hunger / Energy / Cravings. The simplified three-marker check for real-time metabolic state.

Clinical use for BMR: Track SHMEC as a secondary indicator. When a client reports sleep improving and cravings reducing, the identity-level work is landing in the body.

Clinical Application for BMR

Compulsive sexual behaviour as a metabolic-level stress response

The Soother cycle in the body

Compulsive sexual behaviour is a Soother-archetype metabolic pattern: the Pleasure drive running without Purpose as the organising principle. The body is seeking cortisol relief. The behaviour temporarily delivers it. The shame that follows reinstates the cortisol. The cycle perpetuates.

Breaking the cycle

  1. Rewrite: identity-level work to address the underlying AFRAID baseline
  2. Rewire: BEEP and breath sessions to release the emotional charge from the body (working directly on the HPA axis, not just the mind)
  3. Retrain: new behavioural patterns that provide cortisol regulation through legitimate channels, exercise, community, honest relationship

The replacement pattern warning: Suppressing one numbing behaviour without addressing the AFRAID baseline simply shifts the Soother archetype to the next available outlet. This is why willpower-based recovery fails and identity-based recovery works.

The reframe that matters: Recovery that improves sleep, exercise, and stress reduction is not a lifestyle bonus. It is a direct metabolic intervention that reduces the physiological pull toward numbing behaviour. When SHMEC improves, urge frequency decreases, not through willpower, but through biology changing the conditions that generate the urge.
